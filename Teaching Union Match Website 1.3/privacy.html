Privacy

Privacy, in plain English.

The short version: there's no sign-up, we never ask who you are, and the only data we collect is anonymous and aggregated — and only if you say yes.

What we ask you for

Nothing that identifies you. There's no account, no sign-up, and we never ask for your name, email address or phone number. You can use the whole tool and close the tab without telling us who you are.

Your quiz answers

While you take the quiz, your answers are saved to your own browser (in local storage) so you can close the tab and pick up where you left off. That copy lives on your device, not with us, and you can clear it any time by clearing your browser data.

What we measure — only if you agree

When you first visit, we ask whether we can measure how the tool is used. If you decline, nothing is recorded and the tool works exactly the same. If you accept, we collect anonymous, aggregated information to help us spot bugs and improve how accurate and useful the tool is:

  • Which pages are visited and how the quiz flows
  • Which questions get answered, and the general pattern of answers
  • Which options people click through to
  • An approximate location (country or region), worked out from your IP address — never a precise location
  • Basic device information, such as browser type and screen size

This is handled for us by Google Analytics, acting as our data processor. It's only ever looked at in aggregate — we cannot, and do not, single out an individual person from it.

Cookies

By default the site sets no analytics cookies. A single analytics cookie is only ever set after you choose "Accept" on the banner. Your choice itself is remembered in your browser so we don't keep asking. You can change your mind any time by clearing the site's data in your browser.

What we never do

  • We never sell or rent your data to anyone
  • We never use it to track you across other websites
  • We never contact you — there are no marketing emails or texts, because we don't have your details
  • We earn no commission and have no affiliate links, so we have no reason to push you toward any particular organisation

Your choices and your rights

You can decline analytics at any time, and the tool works just the same. Because we don't collect anything that identifies you, there's no personal profile to request or delete. If you have any question about privacy, or want us to explain anything here, email info@edapt.org.uk and we'll happily help.

Changes to this page

If we change how the tool handles data, we'll update this page. The current approach is the one described above.
